  • Santoryu

    Zoro is a master swordsman known for his unique Three-Sword Style, where he wields a sword in each hand and one in his mouth. This unorthodox style allows him to perform devastating and versatile attacks.

  • First Crew Member

    Zoro was the first person to join Monkey D. Luffy’s crew. He initially agreed to join after Luffy saved him from execution and helped retrieve his swords.

  • Ambition

    Zoro's ultimate goal is to become the world's greatest swordsman, a title currently held by Dracule Mihawk. This ambition drives his relentless training and fighting spirit.

  • Sense of Direction

    Despite his incredible combat skills, Zoro is infamous for his abysmal sense of direction, often getting lost even in straightforward situations. This trait is a running gag throughout the series.

About

RORONOA ZORO

Roronoa Zoro, a fan-favorite character from One Piece, is the fiercely loyal swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and a master of Santoryu, the Three-Sword Style. Introduced as a bounty hunter, Zoro dreams of becoming the world’s greatest swordsman, a promise made to his late friend and rival, Kuina. His dedication to his craft and unwavering loyalty to Captain Monkey D. Luffy make him a pillar of strength in the crew.

Zoro wields legendary swords like Wado Ichimonji, Sandai Kitetsu, and Enma, showcasing incredible skill and determination in battles. Despite his stoic demeanor, Zoro has a humorous side, often getting lost or engaging in lighthearted disputes with Sanji. His resilience is unmatched, enduring severe injuries for his crew’s sake, exemplified by his iconic moment at Thriller Bark.

A symbol of honor and perseverance, Zoro’s journey is a testament to his unyielding spirit, making him an enduring icon in the world of anime.
