My journey with Zoro

Before coming to Japan in 2022, I had never watched anime or explored its fascinating worlds. It wasn’t until a friend introduced me to One Piece that my journey into anime truly began. Among all the characters, Roronoa Zoro captivated me with his unwavering dedication, strength, and sense of loyalty. His relentless pursuit of his dream to become the greatest swordsman deeply resonated with me, inspiring me to approach my own goals with the same determination. Zoro’s resilience and honor have made him not just a character I admire, but a true source of inspiration in my life.
